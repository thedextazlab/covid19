What you need to do:

Unachotakiwa kufanya:

🧼Wash your hands frequently

🧼Nawa mikono mara kwa mara

👄Avoid touching your eyes, mouth and nose

👄Usishike macho, mdomo na pua

💪Cover your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze

💪Ziba mdomo wako na pua kwa mkono uliokunjwa au tishu unapokohoa au kupiga chafya

🚷Avoid crowded places

🚷Kaa mbali na sehemu zenye mikusanyiko

🏠Stay at home if you feel unwell - even with a slight fever and cough

🏠Kaa nyumbani kama haujisikii vizuri - hata kama una homa na kikohozi kiasi

🤒If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical care early - but call by phone first

🤒Kama unapata homa, unakohoa na unapumua kwa shida, wahi mapema kupata huduma ya afya - lakini piga kwanza simu

ℹ️Stay aware of the latest information from WHO and your local authorities

ℹ️Fuatilia taarifa mpya zinazotolewa na Shirika la Afya Duniani (WHO) na mamlaka husika karibu nawe
